About Paws & Hearts

Founded in 2025, Paws & Hearts has helped over 5,000 pets find their forever homes. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need while promoting responsible pet ownership.

We believe every animal deserves love, care, and a safe home. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure each pet receives the attention and medical care they need before being placed with a loving family.
